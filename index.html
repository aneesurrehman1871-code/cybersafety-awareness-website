What is Cybersecurity?

Cybersecurity refers to the practice of protecting computers, networks, programs, and data from unauthorized access, damage, or attacks. In today's digital world, where students use the internet for learning, communication, and entertainment, understanding cybersecurity is essential.

Why is Cybersecurity Important for Students?

Students are frequent targets of cyber attacks because they often:

  • Use weak or easily guessable passwords
  • Share personal information on social media without privacy settings
  • Click on suspicious links or download unknown files
  • Use public Wi-Fi networks without protection
  • Fall victim to phishing scams and fake websites

Project Objective

This website aims to:

  • Educate students about common cyber threats
  • Provide practical safety tips for secure internet use
  • Raise awareness about protecting personal information online
  • Promote responsible digital citizenship

Note: This is an educational project. No personal data is collected or stored.
